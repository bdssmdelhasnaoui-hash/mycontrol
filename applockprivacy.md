# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 02, 2026

**AppLock: Fingerprint & Vault** (referred to as "the App") is developed by **Pranav Purwar** ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App.

---

## 1. Information Collection and Use

The App is designed to protect your privacy. We do **not** collect, store, or share any of your personal or sensitive data. All processing related to app locking and security happens locally on your device.

### Permissions Used and Their Purpose:

To provide its core functionality, the App requires several permissions:

*   **Accessibility Service API:** Used solely to detect when a protected app is launched or closed to show the lock screen. No personal or sensitive data is collected, stored, or shared.
*   **Usage Stats (PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS):** An alternative method to detect app foreground changes for locking purposes.
*   **System Alert Window (Display Over Other Apps):** Required to display the lock screen overlay on top of protected applications.
*   **Device Administrator:** Used to prevent unauthorized uninstallation of the App, ensuring your protection remains active.
*   **Biometric/Fingerprint (USE_BIOMETRIC):** Used to allow you to unlock apps using your device's biometric sensors. The App does not have access to your actual biometric data; it only receives confirmation of successful authentication from the system.
*   **Query All Packages:** Used to list all installed applications on your device so you can select which ones to protect.
*   **Post Notifications:** Used to show persistent notifications to ensure the background protection service remains active and reliable.
*   **Receive Boot Completed:** Allows the protection service to start automatically when your device restarts.
*   **Request Ignore Battery Optimizations:** Used to prevent the Android system from killing the background protection service, ensuring consistent security.
